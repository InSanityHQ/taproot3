\begin{itemize}
\item Don't cause personal harm to self or others
\item Plan everything, write everything down, detail all changes
\item Disclose information responsibly with appropriate, official channels
\item (from the original list) Don't act offensively for financial gain
except where the act of an attack itself is agreed to by the
proprietor of the system to directly bring financial gain under
applicable law
\item If something is in a gray zone, ask, research, until its not in a gray
zone
\item Don't hide information found unless the act of releasing it will break
another rule
\end{itemize}
