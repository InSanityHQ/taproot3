\subsection{Before we start: Monarchies in Europe}
\label{sec:org3ad108c}
"Old Europe" monarchies were mostly absolute before the 18th century.
Crown passed to the eldest son or daughter. England's Glorious
Revolution paved the way for new monarchies to develop.

\subsubsection{Glorious revolution}
\label{sec:orgd1591c6}
\begin{itemize}
\item 1688 struggle between parlament vs. stuart kings result in
constitutional monarchy in England
\item Powers of European monarchs increased after centralization of power
\item Created unified modern states
\end{itemize}
