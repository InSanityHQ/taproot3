Dot Product

dot productdef

For $x, y \in \RR ^n$, the dot product of $x$ and $y$, denoted $x \cdot y$, is defined by

\[\begin{aligned} x\cdot y = x_1 y_1 + \cdots + x_n y_n \end{aligned}\]

where $x= x_1, \ldots, x_n$, $y = y_1, \ldots, y_n$

to work with complex vectors, we define the inner product.
