Polynomials of Operators

$p(T)$def

Suppose $T \in \mathcal{L} (V)$ and $p \in \mathcal{P} (\mathbb{F} )$ is a polynomial given by

\[\begin{aligned} P(z) = a_0 + a_1z + a_2z^2 + \cdots + a_m z^m \end{aligned}\] for $z \in \mathbb{F} $. Then $p(T)$ is the operator defined by \[\begin{aligned} p(T) = a_0I + a_1T + a_2T^2 + \cdots + a_m T^m \end{aligned}\]

\[\begin{aligned} p \mapsto p(T) \end{aligned}\] is linear
