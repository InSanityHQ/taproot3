=> Instantaneous rate of change at a particular point

  • Average rate of change = \(\frac{\Delta Y}{\Delta X}\)

  • Instantaneous rate of change = \(\lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\Delta Y}{\Delta X}\)
