\begin{itemize}
\item Land heats up quickly, water does not heat up as quickly due to
specific heat capacity difference

\item Results in big land-to-ocean differential

\item Water vapor gets trapped on land because of temperature differential

\item Smoke is trapped above marine layer

\item Sadness!

\item Normal force to lift smoke away is to lift wind up

\item Given that the wind is absent, gravity pulls cloud layer down

\end{itemize}
