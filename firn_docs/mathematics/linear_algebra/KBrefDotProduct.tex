\section{dot product\hfill{}\textsc{def}}
\label{sec:org0c83392}
\begin{quote}
For \(x, y \in \RR ^n\), the \emph{dot product} of \(x\) and \(y\), denoted \(x \cdot y\), is defined by

\[\begin{aligned}
  x\cdot y = x_1 y_1 + \cdots + x_n y_n
  \end{aligned}\]

where \(x= x_1, \ldots, x_n\), \(y = y_1, \ldots, y_n\)
\end{quote}
