Right Handed Coordinate System

right handed coordinate systemdef

\[\begin{aligned} \hat{i} \times \hat{j} = \hat{k} \end{aligned}\]

It follows that

\[\begin{aligned} \hat{i} \times \hat{k} &= -\hat{j} \\ \hat{j} \times \hat{i} &= -\hat{k} \\ \hat{j} \times \hat{k} &= \hat{i} \\ \hat{k} \times \hat{j} &= - \hat{i} \end{aligned}\]

a mnemonic

\[\begin{aligned} \hat{i} \hat{j} \hat{k} \hat{i} \hat{j} \hat{k} \dots \end{aligned}\]

Read it left to right, and the next one will be the cross product. If it's in the wrong answer, then read it backwards and reverse the sign.
