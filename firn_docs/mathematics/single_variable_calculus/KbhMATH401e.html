Step 1: Know what simple interest/compound interest is.

Simple Interest Review

\(P = principle\)

\(r = interest\)

\(t = time\)

\(SI = p \times r \times t\)

Compound interest

\(P = principle\)

\(r = interest\)

\(t = time\)

\(p = compounding\ per\ annum\)

\(CI = P(1+\frac{r}{p})^{tp}\)
