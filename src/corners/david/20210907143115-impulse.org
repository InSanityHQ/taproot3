#+title: Impulse

Impulse is the instantaneous transfer of momentum in the same manner that work is the transfer of energy. 

- $\vec{F}\Delta t = \int \vec{F} dt$
- $\vec{F}$ is defined to be $\frac{d\vec{p}}{dt} = m\vec{a}$.
- $\vec{p} = m\vec{v}$.
