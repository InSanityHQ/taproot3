#+title: Moment of Inertia

|          | Linear            | Rotational             |
| Inertia  | M                 | I                      |
| Velocity | v                 | \omega                 |
| KE       | \frac{1}{2} m v^2 | \frac{1}{2} I \omega^2 |


$I = \sum m_i l_i = \int l^2 dm = \int l^2 \rho dV$ where $\rho$ is density.

#+begin_aside Aside: Density
- $\rho$ is volume density, in units of kg/m^3
- $\sigma$ is surface/area density (think density of sheet of paper) kg/m^2
- $\lambda$ linear density (think density of mass over a wire) kg/m
#+end_aside
