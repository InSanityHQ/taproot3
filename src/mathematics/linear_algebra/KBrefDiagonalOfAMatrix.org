#+TITLE: Diagonal of a matrix
* diagonal of a matrix                                                  :def:
  The diagonal of an /n/-by-/n/ matrix is all the entries of the form $a_{i,i}$ for $0 < i \leq n$
  \[\begin{aligned}
  \begin{pmatrix}x &&\\&\ddots&\\&&x\end{pmatrix}
  \end{aligned}\]
