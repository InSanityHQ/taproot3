#+TITLE: v* conjugate transpose
* conjugate transpose, v*                                               :def:

  The conjugate transpose ($v^*$) unary operator means you conjugate a vector and then take the transpose

  \[\begin{aligned}
  v^* = \overline{v}^\top
  \end{aligned}\]
