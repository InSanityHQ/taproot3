* Tiling the Pringlehouse

\begin{equation}
   \begin{cases}
   x(t) = 30cos(t)\\ 
   y(t) = 20sin(t)\\ 
\end{cases}
\end{equation}

and the roof is defined by:

\begin{equation}
r(x,y) = \frac{1}{400}\left(\sqrt{3}x-y\right)^2 - \frac{1}{400}\left(\sqrt{3}y-x\right)^2 + 10
\end{equation}
